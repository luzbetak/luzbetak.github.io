Help me plan and optimize my week.

Phase 1: Calendar Review

Review my calendar and show me a summary:

  • Total meetings
  • Busiest days
  • Where I have gaps of 2+ hours

Phase 2: Clarifying Questions

Before proposing changes, ask me about:

  • What I'm trying to accomplish this week
  • How much focus time I need and for what
  • Any deadlines or commitments not on my calendar
  • Which types of meetings I should decline or shorten
  • Personal commitments or boundaries I want to protect

  • Focus blocks to add
  • Meetings to decline or reschedule
  • Time conflicts to resolve

Prioritization Strategy

Start with the highest-impact changes first. For each proposal, include:

  1. What change you're recommending
  2. Why it will improve your week
  3. Specific actions needed to implement it

Phase 4: Implementation

Once I approve each change, make the edits directly in my calendar one at a time.

Key Process Notes

  • Do not make any calendar changes until I explicitly approve them
  • Present all proposed changes before making edits
  • Execute approved changes sequentially, confirming each one
  • Surface any conflicts or concerns that arise during implementation
